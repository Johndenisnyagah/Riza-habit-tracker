# Palette's Journal - Critical UX/Accessibility Learnings

## 2025-05-14 - [Password Visibility & Focus Accessibility]
**Learning:** Users often misspell passwords in floating-label input designs. Providing a visibility toggle significantly reduces login friction. Additionally, standard focus outlines can be too subtle against blurred glass backgrounds; a more prominent, high-contrast outline is necessary for WCAG 2.1 AA compliance in glassmorphic interfaces.
**Action:** Always include a password visibility toggle for authentication forms and use 2px-3px solid high-contrast outlines for focus states in the "Riza" design system.
